track your nutrition

Built for simplicity, designed for results.

Calorie & Macro Rings

Beautiful animated rings show your daily progress at a glance. Track calories, protein, carbs, and fat with real-time updates.

Weekly Trends

7-day bar charts with weekly averages. See your consistency over time and identify patterns in your nutrition.

Barcode Scanner

Scan any food product barcode to instantly look up nutrition facts from the Open Food Facts database.

Custom Recipes

Create recipes with multiple ingredients and servings. Log a whole meal in one tap with automatic macro calculations.

Streak Tracking

Build consistency with a daily logging streak. Get personalized motivational messages based on your daily progress.

Quick Add

Re-log recent meals with a single tap. Your frequently eaten foods are always just one tap away.

1

Set Your Goals

Choose from preset profiles (Cut, Maintain, Bulk) or set custom calorie and macro targets. Use the protein calculator for precise protein goals.

Log Your Meals

Search from thousands of foods, scan barcodes, create custom foods, or use your saved recipes. Adjust servings with a tap.

3

Hit Your Targets

Watch your rings fill up throughout the day. Check weekly trends, maintain your streak, and share progress via iMessage.
